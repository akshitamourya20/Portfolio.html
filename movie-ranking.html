The Best Movies According to Akshita

My Top 3 movies of all time

1. Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

A beautiful story based on friendship which turns out to be a romantic and love story.


2. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Again its a story of 3 friends who enjoy their life without thinking of the future. To live their life at its best in the present.


3. Friends

It is the most beautiful and funny web series which tells us about the best of living and enjoying with friends, loving them etc
